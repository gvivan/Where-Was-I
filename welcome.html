Welcome to Where Was I

The extension that helps you track your progress through series and episodes on streaming platforms.

The "Where Was I" folder has been automatically restored after deletion. Your episode tracking will continue working normally. Note: To permanently remove this folder, please disable or uninstall the extension instead of deleting it directly.

How to Use:

  1. Find a show you're watching - on any streaming service.
  2. Use Chrome's bookmark button (the star icon) in the address bar to bookmark the episode.
  3. Select the "Where Was I" folder when saving your bookmark. This special folder has been created for you.
  4. That's it! The extension will track which episode you're on.
  5. To return later, simply open the bookmark from your bookmarks bar or manager. You'll be returned to the episode.
